Bookie: Sell tickets for bets on competitions and pay rewards automatically

Bookie allows you to manage bets between friends on competitions. Each user chooses an option to bet on, fills a lightning-address to receive the winning reward, and sends an amount of sats. Bookie comes with a shareable ticket scanner, which can be used to quickly find a ticket (i.e. in case of a dispute).
Competition States:
- INITIAL: Winning choice was not selected yet by the manager.
- COMPLETED_PAYING: Winning choice was selected by the manager or the manager decided to cancel the competition - iterating over users to try to pay rewards or refunds.
- COMPLETED_PAID: Iterating over the users has completed.
Ticket States:
- INITIAL: Winning choice was not selected yet.
- LOST: Ticket choice has lost.
- WON_UNPAID: Ticket choice has won, iteration over winning users has not reached this user yet.
- WON_PAYING: Ticket choice has won, trying to pay reward to user.
- WON_PAYMENT_FAILED: Reward payment failed.
- WON_PAID: Reward payment succeeded.
- CANCELLED_UNPAID, CANCELLED_PAYING, CANCELLED_PAYMENT_FAILED, CANCELLED_PAID: Same as WON_UNPAID, WON_PAYING, WON_PAYMENT_FAILED, WON_PAID, but in case the competition was cancelled and the user deserves to get a refund instead of a reward.
